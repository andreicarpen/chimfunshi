Adopt a Chimp

 

You can make a contribution towards the Chimfunshi Wildlife Orphanage by adopting one of the 7 youngest chimps at the sanctuary. The care and custody of the chimpanzees remains with Chimfunshi, but the pride and satisfaction of contributing to their rehabilitation remain with you.

 

 

Membership

 

The Friends of Chimfunshi is an international organization that was established to create greater awareness of the plight of orphaned chimpanzees and to assist the work being done at the Chimfunshi Wildlife Orphanage in Zambia. Chimfunshi is registered as a non-profit charity in the United States and South Africa, and includes support chapters throughout Africa, Europe and North America. Chimfunshi does not receive any government funds or support in Zambia, and relies entirely on grants, donations and public generosity to continue its work.

Gonzaga University at Chimfunshi

 

In the Jesuit spirit of developing “men and women for others,” the Psychology and Biology Departments, in collaboration with the Center for Community Action and Service Learning, offers international service-learning summer academic courses in Zambia, Africa. Zambia, a southern African country, is a “twinned province” with the Oregon Province of Jesuits which includes Gonzaga University. For more information visit Gonzaga University's website.
